# Cart, Checkout, and Account Policy

Cart, checkout, My Account, login, order, and session-dependent URLs are not normal organic landing pages. They require special handling for indexing, crawling, caching, security, and user-flow validation.

SEO recommendations must not break add-to-cart, cart contents, checkout starts, payment flow, login, account access, price display, availability display, or order-related behavior. Concrete tests belong in Runs or approved Tasks.
