# Faceted Navigation Policy

## Decision Matrix

| Factor | Favor indexable facet | Favor noindex/canonical/block control |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Search demand | Verified demand for the precise facet. | No evidence of demand. |
| Inventory depth | Stable selection with enough products. | Thin, volatile, or empty inventory. |
| Stable URL | Clean, crawlable, canonical URL. | Session, sort, tracking, or unstable parameters. |
| Unique content | Unique title, copy, and merchandising. | Duplicate category text or auto-generated pages. |
| Internal linking | Intentionally linked from navigation/content. | Only generated by filter combinations. |
| Crawl demand | Useful crawl path with limited combinations. | High crawl waste or infinite combinations. |
| Duplicate risk | Low overlap with category/product pages. | High overlap, sort duplicates, or parameter explosion. |
| Business value | Clear conversion or revenue priority. | Low commercial value. |

A facet may become an approved landing page only after evidence, Decision approval if needed, Task readiness, validation, and rollback planning.
