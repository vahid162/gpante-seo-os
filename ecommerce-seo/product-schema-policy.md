# Product Schema Policy

Product schema must reflect visible product content and WooCommerce state. Required review areas include product identity, images, offers, price, currency, availability, variations, reviews, aggregate ratings only when genuine, SKU/MPN/GTIN when available and safe, breadcrumbs, and duplicate schema from WoodMart, Yoast, WooCommerce SEO, or custom code.

Never fabricate ratings, review counts, prices, availability, or identifiers.
