# gpante.com SEO Scope

## Included in Repository Guidance

- WordPress content SEO.
- WooCommerce product, category, variation, filter, cart, checkout, account, REST, and AJAX SEO considerations.
- Yoast SEO and WooCommerce SEO governance.
- Technical SEO for Nginx/PHP-FPM/MariaDB/FastCGI cache context.
- Content, authority, measurement, validation, and rollback frameworks.

## Excluded Without Approval

- Production access or modification.
- WordPress administration changes.
- Database, server, DNS, CDN, analytics, or Search Console actions.
- Storage of sensitive or raw production evidence.
