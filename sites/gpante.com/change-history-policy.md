# gpante.com Change History Policy

Record site-specific changes only when they are repository-safe and tied to a Run, Decision, Task, or sanitized source.

## Required Fields

- Change identifier.
- Date observed or implemented.
- Source or related record.
- Fact, inference, or recommendation label.
- Affected URL class or system area.
- Validation status or limitation.
- Rollback reference where applicable.

Do not record credentials, raw logs, customer data, private URLs, or unsupported claims. Historical versions must be labeled as dated snapshots, not permanently current facts.
