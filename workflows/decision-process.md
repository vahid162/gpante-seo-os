# Decision Process

Repository-wide Decisions are owned by `../decisions/` and use status identifiers from `status-definitions.md`.

## Flow

```text
Proposed → Approved
Proposed → Rejected
Approved → Superseded
```

## Requirements

A Decision record must document context, related Run, related Findings, Evidence references or limitations, rationale, alternatives, consequences, related Tasks, and supersession relationships when applicable.

Superseded Decisions remain historical records. Do not delete or rewrite them to hide prior repository direction.

This process does not authorize Production access or implementation. Implementation requires approved canonical Tasks.
